Prepare to Serve

You're excited. You're honored. You've been called to serve the Lord as a full-time temple missionary! One thing you don't want to worry about is where to stay. When you stay with us, everything will be taken care of so you can focus on giving with your whole mind and heart.

The Perfect Setting

  • We pride ourselves on offering great value suites that meet the needs of full-time temple missionaries. With more amenities than our regular rooms, these suites will help you stay focused on your service.
