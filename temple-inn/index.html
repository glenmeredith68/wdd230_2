Portland, Oregon Temple

The Portland Temple is Oregon's largest temple, serving the majority ofthe population of the entire state. Nearby are numerous parks and family-friendly attractions to visit on your trip as well. Made of white marble, the Portland Temple is a sight to behold! The best months to visit are in the summer since that's when the sun is shining to keep the fog away.
