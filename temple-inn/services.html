Who are we?

Temple Inn & Suites provices the finest specialized hotel accomodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. We cater to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing services and accommodations that meet their needs. The hotel accommodations and services aren't just for Latter-day Saints though, anyone is welcome to stay with us and enjoy all that we have to offer!

Kids!

  • An awesome playroom with a jungle gym you can play in for free!
  • The pool is open all day. Check out the water slide!
  • TV's in every room have all the best games and cartoons.

Parents

  • Low-cost, short-term child care provided so you can attend the temple without worrying about watching the kids.
  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for you to use after your long drive.
  • Complimentary temple shuttle every 30 minutes.
