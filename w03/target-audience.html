Target Audience

The target audience for this site is someone who lives in the Southeast Idaho region who is or could be interested in the goings-on of Mud Lake and attractions thereat. The audience also includes businesses looking for potential places to do business, as well as outdoors people looking to see what Mud Lake has to offer.
