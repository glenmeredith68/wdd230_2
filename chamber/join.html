Applicant Information
Membership Benefits
  • Non-profit

    • Non-profit organizations pay no fees.

    • Basic booth placement at our events.

    • Full participation and voice in Chamber meetings. Be heard!

  • Bronze

    • Bronze membership is just $50/year.

    • Basic booth placement at our events, plus access to Chamber resources (decorations, projectors etc.).

    • Quarterly meetings one-on-one with a board member.

  • Silver

    • Silver membership is $150/year: less than a year of Netflix!

    • Central booth placement at our events, plus the same access to Chamber resources as Bronze. You also get your brand name on all event signage.

    • Monthly meetings one-on-one with a board member.

  • Gold

    • Gold membership is $300/year for these benefits:

    • Premium booth placement at our events, full access to Chamber resources, brand name on signage, and naming rights for various events throught the year.

    • Direct access to all the board members, all the time. Get our help any day of the week!
